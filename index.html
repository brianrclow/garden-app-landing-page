About
Garden Guide is an app for learning about gardening, and managing the plants in your garden.
With My Garden Guide you will be able to learn how to plant your favorite fruits and vegetables as well as keep track of them once they are planted.
Here is our Plant Catalog. We have 20+ different plants to pick from and are adding new ones yearly!
Creating and accessing an account is easy with our registration and log in pages!
Tailor your account, access your information, and find our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy from our settings tab
Use the My Garden functionality to add, update, and delete plants, to help you organize, track, and maintain your gardening activities.
You can add plants from the Catalog for easy access to 20+ plants and see and access your personal plants from the My Garden page.
